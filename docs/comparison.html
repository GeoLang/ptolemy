Key Advantages of Ptolemy

AdvantageDetails
Open Source (AGPL-3.0)No per-user licensing fees. Full source access. Community-driven.
Git-like versioningTrue branch/merge workflows vs Esri's limited parent/child versions.

AI-readyBuilt-in pgvector for spatial AI/ML workflows — no equivalent in Esri.
Trajectory supportMobilityDB for moving objects — Esri requires separate Tracker for Location.
